Headings

All HTML headings, <h1> through <h6>, are available. .h1 through .h6 classes (class="h1") are also available, for when you want to match the font styling of a heading but still want your text to be displayed inline.

h1. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

h2. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

h3. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

h4. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

h5. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
h6. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

<h2>h2. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</h2>

<h4>h4. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</h4>

<h6>h6. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</h6>

Subheader

Create lighter, secondary text in any heading with a generic <small> tag or the .text--small class.

h1. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Secondary text

h2. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Secondary text

h3. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Secondary text

h4. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog

Secondary text
h5. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
Secondary text
h6. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
Secondary text

Alignment classes

Realign text to components with text alignment classes.

Left aligned text.

Right aligned text.

Center aligned text.

Justify text.

<p class="text--left">Left aligned text.</p>
<p class="text--right">Right aligned text.</p>

Transformation classes

Transform text in components with text capitalization classes.

Lowercased text.

Uppercased text.

Capitalized text.

<p class="text--lowercase">Lowercased text.</p>
<p class="text--uppercase">Uppercased text.</p>
<p class="text--capitalize">Capitalized text.</p>

Statistics

If you're building a dashboard, you might need to display some important numbers real big. Just add the .text--big class to any element to amp up the font size.

Amount

100
<p>Amount</p>
<div class="text--big">100</div>

Optional

To mark fields as optional.

(Optional) 
<span class="text--optional">(Optional)</span>

Bold

To mark texts as bold.

Jane Doe 
<span class="text--bold">Jane Doe</span>

Smaller

To mark texts as 1.2rd of Form fields.

Jane Doe 
<span class="text--smaller">Jane Doe</span>

Names

Name

jane doe 
<span class="text--name">jane doe</span>
